About Us

Who Are We

Fullerton Christian Students has been a registered club at CSUF and FC since 1976. We hold the faith that is common to all believers. Our goal is to provide a community of believers in Christ who love the Lord Jesus, enjoy Him, and pursue Him.

We get together throughout the week for Bible studies and small groups to read, fellowship, pray, study and hang out together.
