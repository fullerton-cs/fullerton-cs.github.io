FAQs / Got a question to ask us?

Q: Do I have to belong to a certain denomination or group to join the club?

We hold to the common faith and receive all believers in Christ. We are students from all over Southern California coming from various backgrounds. We have found that Christ is our common unique portion which all believers have as the foundation and cornerstone of our fellowship. We focus our fellowship on this wonderful person revealed to us in the Bible and how to know, experience and grow in Him in our daily life and walk.

Q: Do I have to be a Christian to meet with you?

We are happy to meet with other students who would like to learn more about what it means to be a Christian. No one is born a Christian but that we all can receive Christ into us through the hearing of faith and believing in His redemptive death. In this way we receive the most wonderful person in the Universe!

Q: I can’t make all your meetings, is that okay?

Yes, we realize that we are all busy and have obligations and responsibilities that we need to take care of as they come up day by day. As you get to know us you will find that we care for one another at all levels. We are concerned for each other's physical health, mental well being, and of course spiritual growth as well as all the things we are involved with family, school, job and much more. The Christian life is not only a personal journey but one we can learn to share with others and discover the unsearchable riches of Christ in His Body!
